About Probashi Footballers

Tracking the global pipeline of Bangladeshi football talent.

About Me & This Website

Hi there! My name is Shifat, and welcome to my pet project, Probashi-Footballers.com

A quick “about me”: I’m a lifelong football (soccer) fanatic in the US/Canada. My love for the game has taken me on a wild journey, especially after moving to Canada in high school and following the men’s national team- from losing 8-1 to Honduras, to the miracle run to qualifying for the 2022 World Cup in Qatar (which I was there for!).

From that journey, I realised that a key part of Canada’s success was its recruitment of dual-national players. A fan-run website called ‘Canucks Abroad’ tracked this recruitment effort. Taking inspiration from it, I realized that Bangladesh, too, needs its own centralized database, especially after seeing Bangladesh start its own transition by convincing stars like Hamza Choudhury and Shamit Shome to represent the green and red. Because let's face it- it’s hard to believe we have this many Bangladeshi immigrants all over the world, and we can’t find 11 good players to represent the national team.

I first started this website in August 2025, with just a handful of players listed. Thanks to an incredible response from the community on social media, it has now grown to over hundreds, with more to be discovered.

This site is my contribution to the future of Bangladeshi football—a comprehensive, fan-driven database of the diaspora players who could help elevate our national team to new heights.

Data Sources & Methodology

The database is compiled from following sources:

  • Official club websites and rosters
  • Football federation registrations
  • Scouting reports and agent information
  • Social media posts and accounts, such as Bangladeshi Ballers,Bangladesh Footy, Origin Foot and Save Bangladesh Football, etc.
  • Community submissions from supporters like you.

We regularly update our database to ensure accuracy and comprehensiveness.

Database Guide

Birthday and Age: Birth year and Birth Date (Month & Day) are kept in separate columns to accommodate for players where exact birthday is unknown, and allows for sorting based on birth year to check against youth national team competitions' birth year requirements.

Positions: Use 2/3 letter abbreviation for the player's position. If specific outfield position is unknown, it will be represented by FW (Forward), MF (Midfielder) or DF (Defender). For the abbreviation, refer to GiveMeSport's guide on football positions (Link).

Nationality Information:

  • Birthplace: Municipality, State/Province and Country where the Player was born. Optional field.
  • Citizenship: Player's country of citizenship, apart from Bangladesh. Represented by ISO 3166-1 alpha-3 codes.

Current Team Information:

  • Club Name: Official club name. Needs to specify if it is a youth team, i.e. Fullham F.C. U19.
  • Country: Country of the league where this club competes in. This also applies to teams that are located in one country but competes in another country's league (i.e. Canadian MLS teams). Uses FIFA country codes.
  • Tier: Which tier of the country's pyramid the league is in. i.e. League 1 Ontario = 3.

Notes: Used to provide additional information that is not explained by the other columns.

National Team Information:

  • NT: FIFA Country Code (Link) of the National Team that this player has made an appearance for.
  • Tier: Which level of the national team this player has appeared for; i.e. Senior, U21, U19.
  • Caps: Amount of appearances this player has made an appearance for the national team.
  • Gls.: Goals scored by this player for the national team.

Last Update: Date in MM/DD/YYYY when this row was last updated with new information. If there has not been new information in a long time (subjective), it will be marked as "Status Unknown".
